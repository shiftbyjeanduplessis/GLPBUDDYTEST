GLP Buddy FINAL APP replacement pack

Included in this pack
- glp-store.js: shared seeded data model for staging/demo use
- glp-common.js: small UI helper layer
- index.html: updated home with due photo prompt and populated previews
- meals.html: saved plan, meal locking, single-meal regenerate, unlocked refresh, week/month shopping list, email export
- progress.html: dated front/side/back photo check-ins, timeline, compare view, 6-month weight trend
- community.html: lightweight support hub with seeded posts
- exercise.html: populated progression state and session preview
- settings.html: goal weight, medication, dose, injection day, photo reminder frequency

Notes
- This pack is intended for the FINAL APP folder in a staging build.
- It is designed to make the app coherent and populated for review/testing without touching your live users.
- Supabase/auth is intentionally left for the next workstream.
